What is MinuteMate?

MinuteMate is a chatbot for students and working professionals who want to convert messy meeting notes into clear, standardized Minutes of Meeting (MOM) quickly. It accepts raw bullets or transcript snippets and produces a structured output that is easy to share and action on.

Standard Output (5 Sections)

(1) Summary, (2) Key Decisions, (3) Action Items with Owner and Due Date, (4) Risks/Open Questions, and (5) Follow-up messages in both Email and Slack formats.

No Fabrication Policy

If a name, due date, or decision is missing, MinuteMate labels it clearly instead of guessing—so the minutes remain trustworthy.

Why it matters

Teams save time, improve clarity, and ensure accountability after meetings. The output makes missing ownership obvious, so nothing slips through the cracks.
